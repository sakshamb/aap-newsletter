Let’s Strengthen This Movement

by Arvind Kejriwal

It is no secret that the results of the recently completed elections in Punjab, Goa and Delhi were not to our expectations. As an ambitious upstart in the dirty world of Indian politics, the Aam Aadmi Party – your party – has always aimed high.

But in the long arc of history, this is just a blip. We have to remind ourselves how far we have come from where we began. In the last 2 years alone, we have formed a government in Delhi with a historic mandate of 67 out of 70, we have won 22 Assembly seats and become the Opposition party in Punjab, and we now have 49 councillors in Delhi’s municipal corporations.
