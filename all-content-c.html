GST: Concerns of a Common Man
BY MANISH SISODIA

Facebook Live Session by Manish Sisodia on GST on June 9

The Aam Aadmi Party has always supported the GST in-principle. In fact, the Delhi government has made every effort to assist and support Delhi’s traders and businessmen in adapting to this new tax regime including a Facebook live session conducted by Manish Sisodia, Deputy CM and Finance Minister of Delhi, to address concerns with GST implementation. However, GST in its present form suffers from severe flaws. Read this op-ed in the MINT newspaper to learn why Manish Sisodia thinks that, after demonetisation, GST is yet another hasty reform that could imperil the economic stability of the country.
