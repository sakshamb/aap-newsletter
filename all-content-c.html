AAP to enter the Rajya Sabha

AAP to enter the Rajya Sabha
Tweetq
Share

Aam Aadmi Party is set to enter the Rajya Sabha with its first three Members of Parliament (MPs) in the Upper House. The party now has a presence in both Houses of Parliament apart from having legislators in two state Assemblies and representatives in several municipal bodies across the country.

The three MPs have the best combination of activism, domain knowledge, and political capability. The three will be a strong voice in Parliament, taking up issues of Delhi as well as India. Click on the name of the MP below to read their complete profiles:

Sanjay Singh, member of the Political Affairs Committee of the AAP and a National Spokesperson. He brings deep political knowledge of all relevant issues.

Narain Dass Gupta, a Chartered Accountant, and a well-known expert on tax policy. He can highlight the issues facing traders and businesses.

Sushil Gupta,a businessman and social worker, who has dedicated his life to providing education and healthcare. He can highlight the party’s work in these fields and take up related issues.

Delhi government proposes to bear costs of road accident victims

Tweetq
Share

The #DelhiModel of governance is of compassion and care for citizens. Often victims of the nearly 8,000 road accidents a year in Delhi are poor and passers-by are unwilling to take them to a nearby hospital. To help victims in their “golden hour”, the Delhi government has proposed to pay all the costs of emergency treatment at any private hospital in Delhi of any road accident victim. This will save precious time in searching for a Government hospital.  Read more »
